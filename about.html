Apollo Studio


Who we are

Welcome to our recording studio, where music and technology come together to create magic. Our journey started in 2020, when three brothers with a deep passion for music and a vision to bring high-end recording equipment to all musicians, founded this studio.


Where we are

Located in the vibrant city of Manchester, we have established ourselves as a hub for local musicians and music enthusiasts alike. Our state-of-the-art studio is equipped with the latest technology, including the best recording and mixing equipment, to produce the highest quality audio recordings. We work hard to ensure that our studio is not only professional but also comfortable, creating an atmosphere that inspires creativity and fosters a love of music.


Our vision

In addition to recording services, we also offer our favorite albums and merchandise for sale, as a way to share our passion for music with the world. Our goal is to support local musicians and help them reach their full potential. Whether you're an aspiring musician, a band looking to record an album, or a producer searching for the perfect space to create music, our studio has everything you need to bring your visions to life.


Our team

Our team of dedicated professionals is always on hand to provide expert guidance and support. With years of experience and a love of music, they are committed to ensuring that every recording session is a success. We believe that music has the power to bring people together and we are proud to be a part of that community.


Join us

So, if you're ready to take your music to the next level, we invite you to come and experience the magic of our recording studio. Let us help you bring your music to life and share it with the world.
